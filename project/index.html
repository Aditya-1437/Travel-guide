Travel to your DEZIRE

Destination: 7-Wonders

Embark on a journey to discover the awe-inspiring wonders of our world, from the Great Wall of China snaking through rugged mountains to the ethereal beauty of the Taj Mahal. Explore ancient Mayan temples at Chichen Itza, stand at the foot of Christ the Redeemer overlooking Rio, and be humbled by the engineering marvel of the Colosseum. Witness the grandeur of the Great Pyramid of Giza and lose yourself in the mystery of Petra, the rose-red city carved from rock.

Destination: Japan

Immerse yourself in the captivating culture of Japan, where serene temples coexist with neon-lit metropolises. Witness the majestic peak of Mount Fuji, stroll through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, and wander the vibrant markets of Tokyo. Explore the historic sites of Kyoto, soak in steaming natural onsens, and experience the bullet train's exhilarating speed. From the tranquility of ancient shrines to the electrifying energy of pop culture, Japan offers an unforgettable adventure.

Destination: Canada

Explore Canada's vast wilderness, from the majestic mountains of Banff National Park to the crashing waves of Niagara Falls. Hike through glacier-fed lakes in Yoho National Park, spot whales in the waters off Vancouver Island, and delve into cosmopolitan Toronto's vibrant scene. Discover historic cities like Quebec City, wander the charming French Quarter, and kayak amidst the Thousand Islands. Canada offers an unforgettable adventure for nature lovers, history buffs, and urban explorers alike.
